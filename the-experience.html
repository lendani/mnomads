How Does a Remote Work Program Really Work?

The Remote Life is a program that brings professionals, freelancers and entrepreneurs to work, live and travel the world together. We take care of the research, organization, and booking of your stay, work space, and experience so you can see the world, without leaving your work. We do not provide jobs or remote work but do our best to help the participants setup a remote working lifestyle by engaging with their employers and through focused workshops and meetups.

Who is it for ?

The Remote Life is a community of people who believe there’s a whole new world out there waiting to be explored. Our groups are global, diverse, creative, balanced and usually in the age range 22-35. There’s never a boring moment for someone who’s #LivingTheRemoteLife

You are already one of us if you are:

  • a freelancer who believes in being location independent
  • an Entrepreneur or a startup team building inspiring products
  • a Designer seeking creative inspirations
  • a professional working with a progressive organization
  • on a sabbatical and looking for new ideas
  • reading all this and feeling excited!
