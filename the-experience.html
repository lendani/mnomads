Marrakech is your gateway to culture



How Does a Marrakech Nomads Program Really Work ?

Marrakech Nomads makes sure you have the best experience of your life by helping you be location independent. We provide very good working conditions in the best co-working space in Marrakech. The Coworking space is open 24/7 and has a very fast and reliable Internet. We also make sure you have your daily dose of coffee and hot Moroccan tea. You will also have the opportunity to choose from one of our accommodation options. A hotel room in one of the nicest hotels in the city with access to the swimming pool and daily breakfast. If you wish to live the local life, we make it possible for you to experience the Moroccan life first-hand by living with a host family. The families are generally very welcoming and would be more than happy to allow you to share their food. If you are an apartment person you can live in one of city center apartments. We will make sure you will have your private room with full access to a well equipped kitchen. Marrakech Nomads makes sure that you have a busy schedule full of entertainment, networking and life-lasting memories. As a Marrakech Nomads you will be experiencing the city like a local. We will be there for you around the clock everyday! You will also have access to a myriad of organized trips in and outisde the city. A highlight in your trip will be the diversity dimension that we will bring to your life while staying in Marrakech. A trip to the marvellous Moroccan Sahara will leave a finger print of your soul. The camels, the golden dunes and the clear skyies are yet another fantasy that you will acheive! Essaouira trip at the coastline of Morocco is another experience that we will make sure you experience. The jewish-origin city will dazzle you with its unique architecture and high waves.

Who is it for ?

Marrakech Nomads is a community of people who believe there’s a whole new world out there waiting to be explored. Our groups are global, diverse, creative, balanced and usually in the age range 22-35. There’s never a boring moment for someone who’s #LivingTheRemoteLife

You are already one of us if you are:

  • a freelancer who believes in being location independent
  • an Entrepreneur or a startup team building inspiring products
